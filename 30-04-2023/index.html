About Us

Inspired by the call our honorable prime minister coupled with the dynamic leadership in the state of Andhra Pradesh to create more employment opportunities and develop environment friendly systems within the reach of the common man, we established RoyalEV Series. Our innovations allow us to power the products that are both – eco friendly as well cost effective. RoyalEV’s products are able to cover the long distances at higher speeds at lower cost & emissions.
